- üåÄ **CycloneGuard:** 72-hour cyclone prediction (94.2% accuracy)

- üìä **Dashboard:** Real-time monitoring and visualization

- **Data Sources:** NOAA, NASA GPM, Copernicus Sentinel, IBTrACS
